Cell

Name - Cell
Race - Android
Gender - Male
age - 2-4 years
Hair - None
eyes - Green
status - Android with cells of Nameks, Saiyans, & Freeza's race
like - To fight
dislikes - Goku
          
anime - Dragon Ball Z

  Description


 Cell is an android made by Dr. Gero, that was programmed to kill the androids #16, #17,
and #18, as well as Goku. He was sent to kill the other andriods because they killed Dr. Gero.,
and he was sent to kill Goku because he single handedly destroyed Red Ribon as a boy. Cell has 3
forms, and he changes by absorbing people. He was killed by Gohan.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
                             Fly Mode
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

 No fly mode voçê pode combater no ar sem ser derrubado, a não ser por
golpes que jogam voçê longe, mas golpes que normais sem air.fall e fall
não o derrubam.

Todos os golpes com ( Fly mode ) pode ser feitos no chão ou vôo

All moves in (fly mode) only in fly mode or ground

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
                         Como Ver o Final?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

 Muito simples apenas termine o Jogo com o Cell no Arcade mode ou Team
Battle com ele como ajudante ou voçê mesmo jogando com ele, mas para ver
os todos quadros do final faça o seguinte.

Vá na pasta /DATA do Mugen abra o arquivo system.def, e lá no final do
arquivo voçê vai ver essa linha ae, remova a palavra Congratulations 
ficando assim e aumentando o pose.time = 300 para 800. pronto


---------------------------------------------------------------------
modo padrão do Mugen

Win screen definition
This is the screen that is shown when you win in arcade mode.
[Win Screen]
wintext.text = Congratulations
wintext.font = 2,0,0
wintext.offset = 159,70
wintext.displaytime = -1
wintext.layerno = 2
fadein.time = 32
pose.time = 300
fadeout.time = 64

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
                             Especiais
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

* throw = F + (b) ( close ou perto )
 
* Fly Mode = (y+x) ( no ar ou chão)

* KI power Up = segure (Start) ( Fly mode )

* Power Attack = D, DF, F + (b)

* Cell jr. Attack = F, B, F + (x) ou (a)  

* Cell Combo = D, DB, B + (b)

* Levitation Slicer = D, DB, B + (y)

* Negative arrow = D, DB, B + (y) (no pulo)

* Energy Release = (a+b) obs: também destroe magias

* Giga Shoulder =  F + (y+b) ou B + (y+b)

* energy dan = D, DF, F + (y) (fly Mode)

* energy dan reto no fly = D, DB, B + (y) (fly Mode)

* Katssuu Energy Dan = F, B, F + (y) (Fly Mode)

* KameHame ha = D, DF, F + (x) ou (a) (Fly Mode)

* Cho Kamehame Ha = D, DB, B, F + (x) ou (a) (Fly Mode)
